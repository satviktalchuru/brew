Privacy Policy for Brew

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Brew ("the App") is a coffee ranking and logging app. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. Brew does not sell your data, and does not use advertising or analytics SDKs.

Information We Collect

DataWhy we collect itShared with others?
Email address & password Used to create and secure your account (via Supabase Authentication) No — never shown to other users
Username & display name Identifies you to friends inside the App Yes — visible to other users if your profile is public or to your friends
Coffee logs (drink name, brew method, ratings, notes, flavor tags) Core app functionality — builds your personal ranking and taste profile Visible to friends depending on your privacy settings
Wishlist items Lets you save coffees or shops you want to try No
Friend connections, chat requests, likes Powers the social features of the App Yes — visible to the relevant users involved
Blocked users & reports Safety tools to let you block or report other users No — only used internally for moderation
Precise location (while using the App) Used only on your device to show nearby coffee shops and calculate distances via Apple MapKit No — your location is never sent to or stored on our servers

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not use any advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, camera, or microphone.
  • We do not send push notifications from a server — reminders inside the App are scheduled locally on your device.
  • We do not track you across other companies' apps or websites.

Where Your Data Is Stored

Account and app data is stored securely with Supabase, our backend provider, and protected by row-level security so that only you (and, where applicable, your friends) can access it. Your login session is stored locally on your device in the iOS Keychain.

Your Choices

  • Account deletion: You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings → Delete Account. This action is immediate and cannot be undone.
  • Profile visibility: You can control whether your profile is public or friends-only from Settings.
  • Blocking & reporting: You can block or report any user directly from their profile.

Children's Privacy

Brew is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
